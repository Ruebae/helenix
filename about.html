About the University

The University of Zimbabwe was established in 1952 and has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence in Southern Africa.

It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields such as medicine, engineering, humanities, and sciences.

  • Faculty of Medicine
  • Faculty of Law
  • Faculty of Engineering
  • Faculty of Social Sciences
  • Faculty of Education

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education, research, and innovation that contributes to national development.
